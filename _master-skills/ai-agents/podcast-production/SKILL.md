# Podcast Production

## Overview

Podcast production encompasses the creative and strategic planning that transforms ideas into compelling audio content. This skill covers format development, episode planning, guest preparation, show notes creation, and the content direction that shapes podcast identity.

While audio engineering handles the technical aspects, podcast production focuses on the content strategy and creative planning that makes podcasts worth listening to.

### Core Competencies

- Show format development
- Episode structure and planning
- Guest research and interview preparation
- Content calendar management
- Show notes and metadata
- Audience engagement strategy
- Cross-platform content adaptation
- Brand voice for audio

## When to Use

### Primary Applications

1. **Show Development**
   - New podcast launch
   - Format redesign

   - Spin-off development

2. **Episode Production**
   - Episode planning
   - Guest preparation
   - Interview development

3. **Content Expansion**
   - Season planning

   - Cross-promotion

4. **Support Content**
   - Show notes creation
   - Transcript editing
   - Social content
   - Newsletter integration

### Trigger Conditions

- New podcast initiative
- Existing show needs refresh
- Guest podcast needed
- Content marketing expansion
- Thought leadership platform needed
- Community building through audio

## Common Pitfalls

### Content Mistakes

| Pitfall | Problem | Prevention |
|---------|---------|------------|
| Inconsistent schedule | Audience loss | Batch production |
| Too long/unfocused | Listener drop-off | Tight editing |
| Poor guest prep | Weak interviews | Thorough research |
| No structure | Rambling content | Use outlines |

### Production Issues

| Pitfall | Problem | Prevention |
|---------|---------|------------|
| Audio quality | Unprofessional sound | Equipment standards |
| Slow publishing | Lost momentum | Production pipeline |
| Missing show notes | SEO loss | Template process |
| No promotion | Limited reach | Promotion checklist |

### Strategic Problems

| Pitfall | Problem | Prevention |
|---------|---------|------------|
| Unclear audience | Generic content | Define persona |
| No differentiation | Crowded market | Unique angle |
| Unsustainable pace | Burnout | Realistic cadence |
| No cross-promotion | Siloed content | Repurpose strategy |
