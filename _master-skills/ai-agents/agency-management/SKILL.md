# Agency Management Skill

## Core Workflow


> Master external creative partner relationships with vendor selection, SOW development, performance management, and relationship optimization.

## Skill Overview

This skill provides expertise in managing external creative agencies and vendors effectively. It covers partner selection, contract management, relationship building, and performance optimization.

## Core Capabilities

### Vendor Selection
- RFP development
- Agency evaluation
- Capability assessment
- Cultural fit analysis
- Reference checking
- Negotiation strategies

### Contract Management
- SOW development
- Rate negotiations
- Terms and conditions
- Change orders
- Renewals
- Termination procedures

### Relationship Management
- Onboarding processes
- Communication frameworks
- Feedback systems
- Performance reviews
- Conflict resolution
- Partnership building

## Agency Selection Process

### Selection Framework
```yaml
selection_phases:
  phase_1_discovery:
    duration: "2 weeks"
    activities:
      - Define needs and scope
      - Establish budget range
      - Set selection criteria
      - Create long list
    outputs:
      - Requirements document
      - Budget parameters
      - Evaluation criteria
      - 10-15 agency long list

  phase_2_rfp:
    duration: "4 weeks"
    activities:
      - Develop RFP document
      - Distribute to long list
      - Q&A period
      - Receive submissions
    outputs:
      - RFP responses
      - Initial evaluation
      - Short list (3-5 agencies)

  phase_3_presentations:
    duration: "2 weeks"
    activities:
      - Schedule presentations
      - Agency capabilities review
      - Creative tissue session
      - Team chemistry assessment
    outputs:
      - Presentation scores
      - Team feedback
      - Final candidates (2-3)

  phase_4_selection:
    duration: "2 weeks"
    activities:
      - Reference checks
      - Negotiation
      - Contract finalization
      - Announcement
    outputs:
      - Selected agency
      - Signed contract
      - Onboarding plan
```
