# Packaging Design

## Overview

Packaging design creates the physical container and visual presentation for products, serving both functional and marketing purposes. This skill encompasses structural design, graphic design, material selection, and production coordination to create packaging that protects products, communicates brand value, and enhances the customer experience.

Packaging is often the first physical touchpoint with a brand and plays a critical role in purchase decisions, especially at retail. This skill addresses the complete packaging development process from concept to production.

### Core Competencies

- Structural packaging design
- Dieline creation and adaptation
- Graphic design for 3D surfaces
- Material and substrate knowledge
- Print production for packaging
- Unboxing experience design
- Regulatory and labeling requirements
- Sustainability considerations

## When to Use

### Primary Applications

1. **Primary Packaging**
   - Product containers
   - Bottles and jars
   - Boxes and cartons
   - Pouches and bags

2. **Secondary Packaging**
   - Retail display boxes
   - Multi-pack packaging
   - Gift packaging
   - Promotional packaging

3. **Tertiary Packaging**
   - Shipping boxes
   - E-commerce packaging
   - Pallet and case design
   - Protective packaging

4. **Labels and Wraps**
   - Product labels
   - Shrink wraps
   - Sleeve designs
   - Hang tags

### Trigger Conditions

- New product launch
- Product line extension
- Brand refresh requires packaging update
- Cost or sustainability optimization
- Retail channel expansion
- Regulatory compliance needed

## Common Pitfalls

### Design Mistakes

| Pitfall | Problem | Prevention |
|---------|---------|------------|
| Ignoring shelf context | Poor visibility | Test in retail environment |
| Overdesigned | Production issues | Simplify for manufacturing |
| Poor hierarchy | Confusion at shelf | Prioritize key information |
| Wrong color mode | Print color shift | Design in production colorspace |

### Structural Issues

| Pitfall | Problem | Prevention |
|---------|---------|------------|
| Ignoring tolerances | Fit problems | Account for material variance |
| Weak structure | Product damage | Test under real conditions |
| Complex assembly | Production cost | Simplify for automation |
| Wrong material | Performance failure | Test material properties |

### Production Problems

| Pitfall | Problem | Prevention |
|---------|---------|------------|
| Late changes | Expensive revisions | Early prototype approval |
| No press check | Quality issues | Attend press check |
| Wrong dieline | Wasted production | Verify with vendor |
| Missing specs | Incorrect finishing | Complete documentation |
