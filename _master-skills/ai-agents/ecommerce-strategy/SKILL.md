## Overview

E-commerce Strategy encompasses the planning and execution of digital commerce initiatives, from platform selection to omnichannel integration. This skill covers building sustainable online retail operations that compete effectively in the digital marketplace.

### E-commerce Fundamentals

1. **Platform Strategy** - Technology choices and architecture
2. **Channel Strategy** - Where and how to sell
3. **Customer Experience** - Digital shopping journey
4. **Operations** - Fulfillment and logistics
5. **Growth** - Acquisition and retention

## Common Pitfalls

### Strategic Mistakes

1. **Platform Over-Engineering**
   - Building vs buying
   - Premature customization
   - Tech for tech's sake

2. **Channel Conflict**
   - Competing with retailers
   - Inconsistent pricing
   - Poor channel strategy

3. **Customer Neglect**
   - Focus on acquisition only
   - Poor retention effort
   - Ignoring customer feedback

### Operational Errors

1. **Inventory Mismanagement**
   - Stockouts
   - Overstock
   - Poor visibility

2. **Fulfillment Issues**
   - Late shipments
   - High shipping costs
   - Poor packaging

3. **Customer Service Gaps**
   - Slow response
   - No self-service
   - Complex returns

### Technical Issues

1. **Poor Performance**
   - Slow page loads
   - Mobile issues
   - Checkout friction

2. **Integration Failures**
   - Data silos
   - Manual processes
   - Sync errors

## Best Practices

### Strategy

1. **Customer-First** - Build for customer needs
2. **Data-Driven** - Decisions based on evidence
3. **Agile Approach** - Test, learn, iterate
4. **Omnichannel Mindset** - Unified experience

### Technology

1. **Scalable Architecture** - Plan for growth

3. **Performance Focus** - Speed matters

### Operations

3. **Flexibility** - Handle variability

### Growth

1. **Diversified Acquisition** - Multiple channels
2. **Retention Investment** - Keep customers

## Summary

E-commerce Strategy requires balancing technology choices, channel strategies, customer experience, and operational excellence. Success comes from selecting the right platform for your needs, building seamless omnichannel experiences, investing in customer relationships, and maintaining operational efficiency. The most successful e-commerce businesses continuously optimize based on data while remaining agile enough to adapt to market changes.
