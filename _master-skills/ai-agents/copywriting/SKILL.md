# Copywriting

## Overview

Copywriting is the craft of writing persuasive, compelling text that moves readers to action. This skill encompasses everything from punchy headlines to detailed product descriptions, from emotional brand storytelling to conversion-focused calls-to-action.

Effective copywriting combines creativity with strategy, understanding both the art of language and the science of persuasion. This skill covers the complete copywriting process from research and ideation through to final delivery and optimization.

### Core Competencies

- Headline and tagline creation
- Body copy composition
- Call-to-action development
- Tone of voice definition
- Persuasion techniques
- Brand voice consistency
- Conversion optimization
- Cross-platform adaptation

## When to Use

### Primary Applications

1. **Brand Messaging**
   - Taglines and slogans
   - Mission statements
   - Value propositions
   - Brand manifestos

2. **Marketing Copy**
   - Landing page copy
   - Product descriptions
   - Campaign messaging
   - Promotional content

3. **Advertising**
   - Ad headlines
   - Print ad copy
   - Digital ad copy
   - Video scripts

4. **Communications**
   - Website copy
   - Email copy
   - Social media copy
   - PR and press releases

### Trigger Conditions

- New product or service launch
- Brand launch or refresh
- Marketing campaign development
- Conversion rate improvement needed
- Content gaps identified
- Tone of voice inconsistency

## Common Pitfalls

### Writing Mistakes

| Pitfall | Problem | Prevention |
|---------|---------|------------|
| Feature-focused | Doesn't resonate | Lead with benefits |
| Jargon-heavy | Confuses readers | Use plain language |
| Weak CTAs | Poor conversion | Strong action verbs |
| No hierarchy | Wall of text | Structure clearly |

### Strategic Issues

| Pitfall | Problem | Prevention |
|---------|---------|------------|
| Wrong audience | Misses target | Research first |
| Inconsistent voice | Confuses brand | Voice guidelines |
| Ignoring context | Poor fit | Understand placement |
| Over-promising | Trust erosion | Honest claims |

### Process Problems

| Pitfall | Problem | Prevention |
|---------|---------|------------|
| No research | Guessing | Always research |
| First draft final | Weak copy | Edit ruthlessly |
| Too many cooks | Diluted message | Clear approval chain |
| No testing | Assumptions persist | Always test |

## Quality Checklist

### Before Writing

- [ ] Audience clearly defined
- [ ] Key message identified
- [ ] Platform requirements understood
- [ ] Voice guidelines reviewed
- [ ] Competition analyzed

### During Writing

- [ ] Benefits over features
- [ ] Active voice used
- [ ] Reading level appropriate
- [ ] CTAs clear and compelling
- [ ] Structure logical

### After Writing

- [ ] Self-edited for clarity
- [ ] Proofread for errors
- [ ] Brand voice verified
- [ ] Stakeholder approved
- [ ] Formatted for delivery
