## Core Workflow


<essential_principles>

Think of BANT as a medical triage system. Not all injuries are emergencies—same with leads.

**Core Philosophy:**
A lead that has Budget approved, Authority present, Need urgent, and Timeline this quarter is worth 100x more than a random inbound fill-out-a-form lead that's just "exploring options."

**What each letter means:**
- **Budget:** Do they have money? (committed, not hopeful)
- **Authority:** Are we talking to a decision-maker? (or path to one)
- **Need:** Do they have the problem we solve? (real pain, not theoretical)
- **Timeline:** When do they need it? (Q1, specific, not "maybe next year")

**Anti-patterns (what NOT to do):**
- ❌ Spend 5 hours pitching someone with no budget
- ❌ Qualify ONLY on authority (miss growing companies)
- ❌ Skip need discovery (assume our solution fits)
- ❌ Chase every inbound (90% won't close; time is finite)

**Best practices:**
- Score EARLY in conversation (after 10-15 min discovery)
- Ask all four questions BEFORE committing significant time
- Re-qualify if deal stalls (circumstances may have changed)
- Route low-BANT (<2.5/4) to nurture, high-BANT (>3.5/4) to AE immediately

</essential_principles>
