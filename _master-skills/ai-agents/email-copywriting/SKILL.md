# Email Copywriting

## Overview

Email copywriting creates compelling email content that captures attention in crowded inboxes and drives recipients to action. This skill covers the complete spectrum of email types from promotional campaigns to nurture sequences, transactional messages to newsletters.

Effective email copywriting balances persuasion with value delivery, understanding that the inbox is a privileged space where every message competes for attention. This skill addresses both the art of email writing and the science of email optimization.

### Core Competencies

- Subject line optimization
- Email structure and formatting
- Call-to-action design
- Sequence architecture
- Personalization strategy
- A/B testing approach
- Deliverability awareness
- Conversion optimization

## When to Use

### Primary Applications

1. **Marketing Campaigns**
   - Promotional emails
   - Product launches
   - Sale and offers
   - Announcement emails

2. **Nurture Sequences**
   - Welcome series
   - Onboarding sequences
   - Lead nurturing
   - Re-engagement campaigns

3. **Newsletters**
   - Regular updates
   - Content digests
   - Industry insights
   - Company news

4. **Transactional/Lifecycle**
   - Confirmation emails
   - Abandoned cart
   - Review requests
   - Milestone emails

### Trigger Conditions

- Email campaign planned
- Conversion rates need improvement
- Welcome series required
- Newsletter launch
- Re-engagement needed
- Lifecycle emails require optimization

## Common Pitfalls

### Subject Line Mistakes

| Pitfall | Problem | Prevention |
|---------|---------|------------|
| Spam triggers | Deliverability issues | Avoid ALL CAPS, $$$, !!! |
| Clickbait | Trust erosion | Deliver on promise |
| Too long | Truncated on mobile | Under 50 characters |
| Boring | Low open rates | Test bold approaches |

### Body Copy Issues

| Pitfall | Problem | Prevention |
|---------|---------|------------|
| Wall of text | Low engagement | Short paragraphs |
| Weak CTA | Low clicks | Clear, benefit-driven |
| No personalization | Feels generic | Use tokens |
| Too many CTAs | Decision paralysis | One primary CTA |

### Strategic Problems

| Pitfall | Problem | Prevention |
|---------|---------|------------|
| Over-sending | Unsubscribes | Frequency caps |
| No segmentation | Low relevance | Segment lists |
| No testing | Assumptions persist | Always test |
| Ignoring mobile | Poor experience | Mobile-first design |

## Email Formulas

### Subject Line Formulas

```
SUBJECT PATTERNS
├── Benefit: [Benefit] in [Timeframe]
├── Question: Have you tried [solution]?
├── Number: [#] ways to [achieve goal]
├── Curiosity: The [unexpected] way to [result]
├── Personal: [Name], your [thing] is ready
├── Urgency: [Time] left for [offer]
└── Social: [Person/Brand] uses [product]
```

### Email Body Formula (PAS)

```
PROBLEM-AGITATE-SOLVE
├── Problem: Identify the pain point
├── Agitate: Emphasize the impact
├── Solve: Present your solution
├── Proof: Add credibility
└── CTA: Clear next step
```

### Email Body Formula (AIDA)

```
AIDA STRUCTURE
├── Attention: Hook them immediately
├── Interest: Build engagement
├── Desire: Create want
└── Action: Drive the click
```
