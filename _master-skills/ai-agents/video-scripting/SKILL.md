# Video Scripting

## Overview

Video scripting creates the written foundation for video content, translating concepts and messages into visual narratives with dialogue, action, and technical direction. This skill bridges creative ideation and video production, providing the blueprint that guides all production decisions.

Effective video scripting requires understanding both storytelling principles and production realitiesвЂ”what looks good on paper must be achievable on camera. This skill covers the complete pre-production writing process from concept through shooting-ready documentation.

### Core Competencies

- Script structure and format
- Dialogue and voiceover writing
- Visual storytelling
- Storyboard development
- Shot list creation
- Pacing and timing
- Production feasibility
- Revision and collaboration

## When to Use

### Primary Applications

1. **Marketing Videos**
   - Brand videos
   - Product videos
   - Explainer videos
   - Promotional content

2. **Advertising**
   - TV commercials
   - Digital ads
   - Social video ads
   - Pre-roll content

3. **Corporate Content**
   - Training videos
   - Internal communications
   - Event videos
   - Testimonial videos

4. **Content Marketing**
   - YouTube content
   - Social media videos
   - Podcast video
   - Webinar content

### Trigger Conditions

- Video production planned
- Complex message needs visualization
- Stakeholder alignment needed
- Budget requires pre-planning
- Multi-video series needed
- Professional production engaged

## Common Pitfalls

### Writing Mistakes

| Pitfall | Problem | Prevention |
|---------|---------|------------|
| Overwriting | Too much dialogue | Show don't tell |
| Weak opening | Lost viewers | Hook in 3 seconds |
| No clear CTA | No action taken | Plan the ending |
| Talking heads | Visual boredom | Plan b-roll/visuals |

### Planning Issues

| Pitfall | Problem | Prevention |
|---------|---------|------------|
| Vague visuals | Production confusion | Detailed storyboard |
| Unrealistic timing | Over-length video | Time everything |
| Ignoring budget | Can't produce | Know constraints |
| No shot list | Shooting chaos | Complete shot list |

### Process Problems

| Pitfall | Problem | Prevention |
|---------|---------|------------|
| Skipping review | Costly reshoots | Multiple reviews |
| No director input | Creative disconnect | Collaborate early |
| Late changes | Budget overrun | Lock script properly |
| Missing assets | Production delays | Complete asset list |

### Script

- [ ] Clear objective stated
- [ ] Opening hooks viewer
- [ ] Message clear and prioritized
- [ ] Dialogue natural and brief
- [ ] Visual variety planned
- [ ] CTA strong and clear
- [ ] Timing verified

### Storyboard

- [ ] Key moments visualized
- [ ] Shot types indicated
- [ ] Text placement shown
- [ ] Transitions noted
- [ ] Timing aligned with script

- [ ] Shot list complete

## Script Templates

### Two-Column Format

```
VIDEO                          AUDIO
--------------------------------------------------
SCENE 1: INT. OFFICE - DAY    VO: Every day, teams
                              struggle with...
WS of busy office.
                              SFX: Office ambience
MS on frustrated worker.
                              MX: Subtle tension

SCENE 2: CU PRODUCT SHOT      VO: That's why we
                              created...
```

### Single-Column Format

```
SCENE 1: INT. OFFICE - DAY

WIDE SHOT of a busy, chaotic office environment.

                    VOICEOVER (V.O.)
          Every day, teams struggle with
          scattered communication...

CUT TO: MEDIUM SHOT of a frustrated worker at their desk.

                    WORKER
          There has to be a better way.
```
