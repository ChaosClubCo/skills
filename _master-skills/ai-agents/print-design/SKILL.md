# Print Design

## Overview

Print design creates tangible marketing and communication materials that exist in the physical world. This skill covers the design and production of printed materials from business cards to large-format signage, with particular attention to production requirements, print specifications, and the unique considerations of physical media.

Unlike digital design, print is permanent once produced—making attention to detail, color accuracy, and print specifications critical. This skill addresses both creative design and technical production requirements.

### Core Competencies

- Layout and composition for print
- Typography for print media
- Color management (CMYK, Pantone)
- Prepress and file preparation
- Paper and substrate selection
- Print production knowledge
- Finishing techniques understanding
- Large format considerations

## When to Use

### Primary Applications

1. **Business Stationery**
   - Business cards
   - Letterheads
   - Envelopes
   - Notecards

2. **Marketing Collateral**
   - Brochures and folders
   - Flyers and postcards
   - Catalogs and lookbooks
   - Direct mail pieces

3. **Promotional Materials**
   - Posters and banners
   - Point-of-sale displays
   - Trade show materials
   - Event signage

4. **Publication Design**
   - Annual reports
   - Magazines and newsletters
   - Booklets and guides
   - Instruction manuals

### Trigger Conditions

- Physical marketing materials needed
- Event or trade show approaching
- Brand launch requires collateral
- Sales team needs leave-behind materials
- Corporate stationery update
- Signage or environmental graphics needed

## Common Pitfalls

### Design Mistakes

| Pitfall | Problem | Prevention |
|---------|---------|------------|
| RGB colors | Dull, shifted prints | Design in CMYK |
| Thin hairlines | Disappear in print | Minimum 0.25pt |
| Wrong black | Muddy or misregistered | Use rich black for large areas |
| Too small text | Illegible | Minimum 6pt, test print |

### Technical Issues

| Pitfall | Problem | Prevention |
|---------|---------|------------|
| No bleed | White edges | Always 3mm+ bleed |
| Low resolution | Pixelated images | 300 DPI minimum |
| Missing fonts | Text reflow | Embed or outline |
| Wrong color mode | Color shifts | Check before export |

### Production Problems

| Pitfall | Problem | Prevention |
|---------|---------|------------|
| Skipping proofs | Costly errors | Always proof |
| Unclear specs | Wrong result | Written specifications |
| Last minute | Rush fees, errors | Plan buffer time |
| Wrong vendor | Quality mismatch | Vet capabilities |
