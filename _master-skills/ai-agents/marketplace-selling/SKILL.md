## Overview

Marketplace Selling involves leveraging major e-commerce marketplaces like Amazon, eBay, and Walmart to reach customers at scale. This skill covers listing optimization, fulfillment strategies, advertising, and performance management across multiple platforms.

### Marketplace Fundamentals

1. **Platform Selection** - Choosing right marketplaces
2. **Listing Optimization** - Content and visibility
3. **Fulfillment Strategy** - Shipping and logistics
4. **Advertising** - Paid visibility
5. **Performance Management** - Metrics and compliance

### Marketplace Landscape

```
Major Marketplaces:
├── Amazon
│   ├── ~50% US e-commerce

│   └── Walmart+ growth

```

## Common Pitfalls

### Strategic Errors

1. **Single Channel Dependency**
   - All eggs in one basket
   - Policy change vulnerability
   - No diversification

2. **Race to Bottom Pricing**
   - Margin erosion
   - Unsustainable competition
   - Brand devaluation

3. **Inventory Mismanagement**
   - FBA stockouts
   - Long-term storage fees
   - Stranded inventory

### Operational Mistakes

1. **Poor Listing Quality**
   - Weak content
   - Bad images
   - Missing keywords

2. **Fulfillment Issues**
   - Late shipments
   - Wrong items
   - Poor packaging

3. **Customer Service Gaps**
   - Slow response
   - No follow-up
   - Negative reviews

### Compliance Issues

1. **Policy Violations**
   - Prohibited content
   - Review manipulation
   - Listing manipulation

2. **Account Suspensions**
   - Performance failure
   - Policy breach
   - Authenticity issues

## Summary

Marketplace Selling requires mastering multiple platforms while maintaining operational excellence and profitability.
