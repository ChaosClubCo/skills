# Creative Calendar Skill

## Instructions


> Master campaign planning and content scheduling with comprehensive calendar management, resource coordination, and cross-functional alignment.

## Skill Overview

This skill provides expertise in developing and managing creative calendars that coordinate campaigns, content production, and marketing activities. It covers planning frameworks, scheduling tools, and alignment processes.

## Core Capabilities

### Campaign Planning
- Annual planning cycles
- Campaign frameworks
- Theme development
- Milestone mapping
- Resource allocation
- Budget phasing

### Content Calendars
- Editorial planning
- Content scheduling
- Channel coordination
- Publication workflows
- Content mix optimization
- Evergreen management

### Cross-Functional Alignment
- Stakeholder coordination
- Dependency mapping
- Review scheduling
- Launch coordination
- Retrospective planning
- Calendar governance

## Planning Framework

### Annual Planning Structure
```yaml
annual_planning_phases:
  strategic_planning:
    timing: "Q4 (prior year)"
    duration: "6-8 weeks"
    activities:
      - Review previous year performance
      - Align with business objectives
      - Define campaign themes
      - Set calendar framework
      - Budget allocation
    outputs:
      - Annual marketing calendar
      - Campaign briefs (high-level)
      - Budget by quarter

  quarterly_planning:
    timing: "6-8 weeks before quarter"
    duration: "2-3 weeks"
    activities:
      - Detail quarterly campaigns
      - Finalize content calendar
      - Assign resources
      - Set milestones
    outputs:
      - Detailed campaign plans
      - Content calendar
      - Resource allocations

  monthly_planning:
    timing: "2 weeks before month"
    duration: "1 week"
    activities:
      - Review upcoming content
      - Confirm schedules
      - Address gaps
      - Adjust as needed
    outputs:
      - Finalized schedules
      - Production assignments
      - Publication calendar

  weekly_planning:
    timing: "End of previous week"
    duration: "2-3 hours"
    activities:
      - Review week ahead
      - Confirm deadlines
      - Address blockers
      - Real-time adjustments
    outputs:
      - Weekly production schedule
      - Priority list
```
