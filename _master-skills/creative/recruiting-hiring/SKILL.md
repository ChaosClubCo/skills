# Recruiting & Hiring

## Overview

Recruiting and hiring encompasses the end-to-end process of identifying workforce needs, attracting qualified candidates, evaluating applicants, and bringing new talent into the organization. Effective recruiting balances speed-to-hire with quality-of-hire while ensuring legal compliance and positive candidate experience.

For SMB business solutions, a well-designed recruiting process reduces mis-hires, builds employer brand, ensures diverse candidate pools, and creates repeatable systems that scale with company growth.

## When to Use

Invoke this skill when:
- Creating or updating job descriptions and postings
- Designing interview processes and scorecards
- Building candidate sourcing strategies
- Managing applicant tracking and pipelines
- Conducting phone screens and interviews
- Performing reference and background checks
- Negotiating offers and compensation packages
- Ensuring hiring compliance (EEO, ADA, etc.)
- Onboarding recruiting technology
- Training hiring managers on interview techniques
- Analyzing recruiting metrics and funnel conversion

### 2. Job Description Development

#### Job Description Structure

```
JOB DESCRIPTION TEMPLATE

HEADER
- Job Title (clear, searchable)
- Department
- Location (remote/hybrid/onsite)
- Employment Type

COMPANY OVERVIEW (2-3 sentences)
- What we do
- Mission/values highlight
- Why join us

ROLE SUMMARY (3-4 sentences)
- Primary purpose
- Key impact areas
- Team context

RESPONSIBILITIES (6-10 bullets)
- Start with action verbs
- Quantify where possible
- Order by importance
- Include % of time if helpful

REQUIREMENTS
Must Have (5-7 items):
- Years of experience
- Required skills
- Education (if truly required)
- Certifications

Nice to Have (3-5 items):
- Preferred skills
- Industry experience
- Additional qualifications

WHAT WE OFFER
- Compensation transparency
- Benefits highlights
- Growth opportunities
- Culture elements

EEO STATEMENT
Standard equal opportunity language
```

#### Job Posting Optimization

| Element | Best Practice | Impact |
|---------|--------------|--------|

#### Employee Referral Program

```
REFERRAL PROGRAM STRUCTURE

BONUS AMOUNTS
- Entry level: $500
- Professional: $1,500
- Senior/Manager: $2,500
- Director+: $5,000
- Hard-to-fill roles: 1.5x multiplier

ELIGIBILITY
- All employees except HR and hiring managers
- Referral must be new to company
- Not already in ATS
- Must stay 90 days for full bonus

PAYOUT SCHEDULE
- 50% at start date
- 50% after 90 days

PROGRAM RULES
- Unlimited referrals
- Self-referrals not eligible
- Rehires case-by-case
- Interns eligible for intern referrals
```

### 4. Interview Process Design

#### Interview Stage Framework

```
STANDARD INTERVIEW PROCESS

STAGE 1: APPLICATION REVIEW (1-2 days)
- Resume screening
- Basic qualification check
- Knockout questions

STAGE 2: RECRUITER SCREEN (30 min)
- Role overview
- Experience validation
- Compensation alignment
- Logistics/timeline

STAGE 3: HIRING MANAGER INTERVIEW (45-60 min)
- Technical/functional depth
- Team fit assessment
- Role-specific scenarios
- Candidate questions

STAGE 4: TEAM INTERVIEWS (2-3 hours)
- Cross-functional perspectives
- Skills demonstration
- Culture assessment
- Peer evaluation

STAGE 5: FINAL ROUND (60 min)
- Executive/skip-level
- Strategic alignment
- Offer discussion prep

STAGE 6: REFERENCE CHECKS
- 2-3 professional references
- Verification of claims
```

### 5. Candidate Evaluation

#### Structured Interview Questions by Competency

```
BEHAVIORAL INTERVIEW BANK

LEADERSHIP
- Tell me about a time you had to influence
  without authority.
- Describe a situation where you had to make
  an unpopular decision.
- How have you developed team members?

PROBLEM SOLVING
- Walk me through a complex problem you solved.
- Describe a time you had incomplete information
  but needed to act.
- Tell me about a mistake and what you learned.

COLLABORATION
- Describe working with a difficult colleague.
- How have you resolved team conflict?
- Tell me about a successful cross-functional project.

ADAPTABILITY
- Describe adapting to a major change.
- Tell me about receiving critical feedback.
- How have you handled competing priorities?

RESULTS ORIENTATION
- What's your proudest professional achievement?
- Describe exceeding a challenging goal.
- Tell me about a time you failed to meet a target.
```

## Common Pitfalls

### Process Failures

1. **Unclear job requirements**: Hiring for wrong profile
2. **Too many interview rounds**: Losing candidates to competitors
3. **Slow decision making**: Top candidates accept elsewhere
4. **Inconsistent evaluation**: Bias in hiring decisions
5. **Poor candidate communication**: Damaged employer brand

### Compliance Risks

1. **Illegal interview questions**: Age, family, religion, etc.
2. **Inconsistent background checks**: Discrimination claims
3. **Missing I-9 verification**: Immigration violations
4. **No offer documentation**: Disputes over terms
5. **Inadequate record retention**: Audit failures

### Strategic Errors

1. **Over-reliance on job boards**: Missing passive talent
2. **Ignoring employer brand**: Reduced candidate quality
3. **Not tracking metrics**: Can't improve process
4. **Hiring for skills only**: Culture mismatches
5. **Negotiating against yourself**: Overpaying unnecessarily
