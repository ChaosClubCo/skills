# Motion Graphics

## Overview

Motion graphics brings static design elements to life through animation, creating engaging visual content that captures attention and communicates complex ideas efficiently. This skill covers the design and production of animated content from logo reveals to full explainer videos, social media content to broadcast graphics.

Motion graphics sits at the intersection of graphic design, animation, and video production, requiring both strong design fundamentals and technical animation expertise.

### Core Competencies

- Logo and brand animation
- Explainer video creation
- Kinetic typography
- Social media motion content
- Title sequences and lower thirds
- UI/UX animation principles
- 3D motion integration
- Template and toolkit creation

## When to Use

### Primary Applications

1. **Brand Animation**
   - Logo animations and reveals
   - Brand identity motion guidelines
   - Animated brand assets
   - Signature transitions

2. **Explainer Content**
   - Product explainer videos
   - Process visualizations
   - Feature demonstrations
   - Tutorial animations

3. **Social Media Content**
   - Animated social posts
   - Story animations
   - Video ad creation
   - Engagement content

4. **Video Production**
   - Title sequences
   - Lower thirds and supers
   - Transitions and effects
   - End cards and CTAs

### Trigger Conditions

- Brand needs animated presence
- Complex concepts require visualization
- Video content requires polish
- Social engagement needs improvement
- Static content underperforming
- Product launches need impact

## Common Pitfalls

### Design Mistakes

| Pitfall | Problem | Prevention |
|---------|---------|------------|
| Overanimation | Distracting, exhausting | Purposeful motion only |
| Poor typography | Unreadable text | Test at delivery size |
| Ignoring audio | Flat, unengaging | Audio-first approach |
| Style inconsistency | Disjointed feel | Style frames approval |

### Technical Issues

| Pitfall | Problem | Prevention |
|---------|---------|------------|
| Heavy files | Slow, crashy projects | Proxy and precomp |
| Bad exports | Quality loss | Test exports early |
| Wrong specs | Delivery rejection | Confirm specs upfront |
| No backup | Work loss | Auto-save and backup |

### Process Problems

| Pitfall | Problem | Prevention |
|---------|---------|------------|
| Skipping storyboard | Misaligned execution | Always storyboard |
| Too many revisions | Budget overrun | Clear approval gates |
| Late audio | Poor sync | Audio first approach |
| Scope creep | Endless project | Defined deliverables |
