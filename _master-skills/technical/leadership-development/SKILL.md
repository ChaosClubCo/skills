# Leadership Development

> Executive coaching programs, succession planning frameworks, and leadership capability building that strengthens organizational leadership capacity.

## Metadata

- **Skill ID**: leadership-development
- **Category**: Back of House - Executive Operations
- **Complexity Level**: Expert
- **Prerequisites**:
  - Leadership experience
  - Talent management understanding
  - Coaching fundamentals
  - Organizational development knowledge

## Overview

Leadership Development encompasses the systematic identification, development, and advancement of leadership talent throughout the organization. This skill covers executive coaching methodologies, succession planning processes, leadership competency frameworks, and development program design that builds sustainable leadership pipelines.

```markdown
## Succession Planning Process

### Critical Role Identification
**Criteria for Critical Roles**
- Strategic impact
- Specialized expertise
- Difficult to fill externally
- High vacancy risk
- Knowledge concentration

**Role Prioritization Matrix**
| Criticality | Vacancy Risk | Priority |
|-------------|--------------|----------|
| High | High | Immediate |
| High | Low | Plan |
| Low | High | Monitor |
| Low | Low | Standard |

### Successor Assessment

**Readiness Levels**
- **Ready Now**: Can step in immediately
- **Ready in 1-2 Years**: Developing critical skills
- **Ready in 3-5 Years**: High potential, needs development
- **Developmental**: Long-term potential

**Assessment Dimensions**
- Leadership competencies
- Technical capabilities
- Track record
- Development trajectory
- Aspiration alignment
- Risk of loss

### Development Planning

**Accelerated Development Actions**
- Stretch assignments
- Executive mentoring
- Cross-functional exposure
- External visibility
- Board exposure
- Acting roles
```

### High-Potential Identification

**High-Potential Assessment**
```markdown
## High-Potential Identification Framework

### Definition
High-potential employees demonstrate the ability and aspiration to advance to significantly more senior roles within the organization.

### Assessment Criteria

**Performance (Table Stakes)**
- Consistent high performance rating
- Exceeds expectations in current role
- Delivers results through others

**Potential Indicators**
| Factor | Indicators |
|--------|------------|
| Learning Agility | Learns quickly, applies to new situations |
| Leadership Capability | Naturally leads, develops others |
| Strategic Thinking | Sees big picture, connects dots |
| Drive | Ambitious, takes initiative |
| Organizational Fit | Embodies values, cultural alignment |

**Assessment Methods**
- Performance history review
- Manager nominations
- Calibration discussions
- Potential assessment tools
- Interview panels

### Calibration Process
1. Initial nominations by managers
2. Data collection and review
3. Cross-functional calibration
4. Leadership approval
5. Development planning
6. Individual communication
```
