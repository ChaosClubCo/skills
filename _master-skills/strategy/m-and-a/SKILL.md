# Mergers and Acquisitions

> Strategic acquisition planning, comprehensive due diligence execution, and seamless integration management for value-creating transactions.

## Metadata

- **Skill ID**: m-and-a
- **Category**: Back of House - Executive Operations
- **Complexity Level**: Expert
- **Prerequisites**:
  - Corporate finance knowledge
  - Strategic planning experience
  - Project management skills
  - Legal and regulatory awareness

## Overview

Mergers and Acquisitions (M&A) encompasses the strategic evaluation, execution, and integration of corporate transactions. This skill covers the complete M&A lifecycle from strategy development through post-merger integration, enabling organizations to grow through acquisitions, divestitures, or strategic combinations.

### 3. Valuation and Deal Structuring

**Valuation Methodologies**
```markdown
## Valuation Approach Summary

### 1. Comparable Company Analysis
- Select peer group
- Normalize financials
- Apply relevant multiples
- Adjust for differences
- Derive value range

**Common Multiples**
| Multiple | Typical Range | Best Used For |
|----------|---------------|---------------|
| EV/Revenue | 1x-10x+ | High-growth, pre-profit |
| EV/EBITDA | 5x-15x | Mature, profitable |
| EV/EBIT | 7x-20x | Capital-light business |
| P/E | 10x-30x | Profitable, stable |
| EV/ARR | 3x-20x+ | SaaS businesses |

### 2. Precedent Transactions
- Identify relevant deals
- Adjust for timing
- Consider deal context
- Apply control premium
- Derive implied value

### 3. Discounted Cash Flow (DCF)
- Project free cash flows
- Determine WACC
- Calculate terminal value
- Discount to present
- Sensitivity analysis

### 4. LBO Analysis
- Model leveraged returns
- Determine entry multiple
- Assess debt capacity
- Calculate IRR
- Set floor valuation

### Valuation Summary
| Method | Low | Mid | High |
|--------|-----|-----|------|

```
