# Competitive Battle Cards

## Purpose
Arm sales teams with competitive intelligence and positioning against major AI platforms.

## When to Use
- RFP asks: "Why you vs OpenAI/Azure?"
- Sales encounter: "We're evaluating multiple vendors"
- Competitive deal with head-to-head comparison

## Key Sections
1. **Strengths vs Competitor** - What we do better
2. **Weaknesses** - Where competitor excels (be honest)
3. **Trap Questions** - Questions that expose competitor gaps
4. **Proof Points** - Customer wins, case studies, benchmarks
5. **Pricing Comparison** - TCO analysis

## Output
2-page battle card per competitor (OpenAI, Azure, AWS Bedrock, Google Vertex)
