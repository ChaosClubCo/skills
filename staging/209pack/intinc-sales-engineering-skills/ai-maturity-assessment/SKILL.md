# AI Maturity Assessment

## Purpose
Evaluate enterprise readiness for AI adoption to identify gaps and de-risk implementations.

## When to Use
- Qualifying $1M+ enterprise deal
- Customer lacks prior AI experience
- Implementation risk assessment needed

## Maturity Dimensions
1. **Data Maturity** - Quality, accessibility, governance
2. **Technical Infrastructure** - Cloud, APIs, data pipelines
3. **Talent & Skills** - ML engineers, data scientists, AI PM
4. **Governance & Policy** - AI ethics, compliance, risk management
5. **Use Case Maturity** - Problem clarity, success metrics defined

## Scoring
Level 1 (Ad Hoc) → Level 5 (Optimizing)
Output: Maturity scorecard + roadmap to next level
