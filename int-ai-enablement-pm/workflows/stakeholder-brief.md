## Stakeholder 1-Pager Brief

**When to use:** First contact with any stakeholder on a new initiative. Default format for Dave, Jackie, Daniel. Executives read 1-pagers, not PRDs.

**Collect before writing:**
- Who is the audience? (Dave / Jackie / Daniel — one brief per person)
- What is the initiative in one sentence?
- What's the current state (baseline)?
- What are you asking them to do? (approve / sign-off / provide resources / attend a demo)
- What does success look like in 30 days?

---

## Brief Template

```markdown
# [Initiative Name] — [Audience] Brief
From: Kyle Rosebrook, Staff Engineer (AI SME) | Date: [YYYY-MM-DD]

---

**What we're doing**
[1 sentence — plain language, no jargon]

**Why it matters to you**
[1–2 sentences — framed to this stakeholder's priorities; see references/stakeholder-map.md]

**What it costs**
[Time: X hours to build, Y hours/month to maintain]
[Budget: $Z if applicable — otherwise "no additional budget required"]

**What we're asking for**
[Specific ask — approval / 15 minutes to demo / sign-off by [date] / resources]

**What success looks like**
[One specific metric + timeline — e.g., "KB article creation time < 10 min within 2 weeks"]
```

---

## After Writing

1. Review against `references/stakeholder-map.md` — does the framing match this stakeholder's priorities?
2. Is the "what we're asking for" specific and actionable? (Not "your support" — a real ask with a deadline)
3. Is the success metric measurable, not aspirational?
4. Chain to `intinc-doc-generator` for INT-branded .docx if needed
