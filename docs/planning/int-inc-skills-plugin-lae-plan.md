# INT Inc. Skills Plugin Portfolio
## Land/Adopt/Expand Strategic Action Plan
**Horizon:** 90 Days (Q1 2026) | **Status:** Full Portfolio Rollout | **Version:** 1.0

---

## Executive Summary

The INT Inc. Skills Plugin portfolio represents a transformational expansion of organizational capability across five strategic skill domains (259 total skills) designed to empower employees and teams with structured learning paths, best practices, and actionable guidance.

**Portfolio Composition:**
- **New Release:** 250 skills across 5 domains (Technical, Strategy, Creative, Industry, Business)
- **Existing Foundation:** 59 skills (Business Skills: 50 | UI/UX Skills: 9)
- **Total Addressable Skills:** 309 complementary skills
- **Target Users:** All teams, departments, and organizational functions

**Strategic Objectives:**
1. Achieve 70% user registration and account activation within 30 days (LAND)
2. Drive 50% engaged users with 3+ skill interactions by day 60 (ADOPT)
3. Scale adoption to 80% active users with cross-team expansion by day 90 (EXPAND)

**Expected Outcomes:**
- Accelerated time-to-competency for new hires
- Improved cross-functional collaboration through shared skill language
- Enhanced employee engagement and retention
- Data-driven capability planning and team composition

## 3. Play Selection: Recommended Adoption Campaigns

### 3.1 Campaign Portfolio

Each campaign is designed as a discrete motion with clear audience, message, mechanics, and measurement framework. Campaigns run in parallel or sequence based on phase.

---

### Play 1: "Skill Superpower" Launch Campaign (LAND Phase)

**Objective:** Drive registration and initial activation through excitement and social proof.

**Target Audience:** All employees; primary focus on early adopter demographic (tech-forward, learning-oriented, influencers).

**Message Pillars:**
- Your superpower is the sum of your skills—discover what you bring to the team
- Growth is a journey—skills portfolio makes it visible and achievable
- Your team is smarter together—shared skill language creates magic

**Campaign Mechanics:**
1. **Teaser Week (Days 4-7):** Slack posts, email headers: "Coming soon: Unlock Your Skill Superpower"
2. **Launch Day (Day 8):** All-hands announcement + email + Slack explosion + in-app welcome modal
3. **First Week Momentum (Days 9-14):**
   - Daily Slack tip: "3 Must-Know Skills in [Domain]"
   - Email sequence: Onboarding journey steps
   - Gamified streak: "Come back tomorrow to unlock [Feature]"
4. **Social Proof (Days 15-21):**
   - Early adopter spotlights: "Meet [Name]—5 skills mastered in 1 week"
   - Manager testimonials: "I used the skills portfolio to identify our team's strengths"
   - Milestone announcements: "We're at 5,000 registered users!"

**Success Metrics:**
- 8,000+ registrations by Day 14 (80% of first-wave target)
- 6,000+ first logins by Day 7
- 50%+ active users by Day 14
- 40%+ of users explore ≥1 domain by Day 21

**Owner:** Communications + Marketing

### Play 2: "Manager Empowerment" Enablement Program (LAND→ADOPT)

**Objective:** Activate managers as primary adoption drivers; provide tools for team conversations and capability planning.

**Target Audience:** All managers and team leads (est. 500-800 people).

**Program Components:**

**Phase 1: "Manager Toolkits" (Days 15-30)**
- Email series: 5-part onboarding (registering, exploring, finding team skills, using in 1:1s, planning development)
- Video tutorials: 3 × 5-minute how-to videos (intro, team assessment, conversation strategies)
- Template downloads: Team skill matrix, 1:1 conversation guide, development plan template
- Success measure: 60% of managers complete email series; 40% use ≥1 template

**Phase 2: "Manager Office Hours" (Days 20-60, ongoing)**
- Weekly 45-min live sessions (3 time zones to accommodate global teams)
- Q&A + best practice sharing + technical support
- Recordings available for asynchronous access
- Success measure: 30+ attendees/session average; 90% Q&A resolution rate

**Phase 3: "Skill Conversation Workshop" (Days 35-50)**
- 60-min live or self-paced module
- Topics: Identifying skill gaps; using portfolio in development conversations; cross-team teaming
- Interactive role-plays and scenario discussions
- Success measure: 60% of managers complete workshop; 50% implement learnings within 2 weeks

**Campaign Mechanics:**
- Incentive: Managers with 80%+ team adoption in ADOPT phase receive "Skill Champion" recognition + exclusive insights dashboard
- Recognition: Spotlight manager success stories weekly in all-hands meetings
- Escalation: Adoption lead 1:1 with underperforming departments (adoption <50%)

**Success Metrics:**
- 60% of managers registered and trained by Day 30
- 50% of active teams using portfolio in 1:1 conversations by Day 45
- 70% manager adoption by Day 60
- Manager NPS ≥65

**Owner:** L&D + Change Management + Adoption Lead

**Issue Severity & Escalation Matrix:**

| Severity | Examples | Response Time | Escalation | Decision Maker |
|----------|----------|----------------|------------|----------------|

