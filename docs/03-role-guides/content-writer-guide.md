# Content Writer / Marketer Guide

This guide curates the most valuable skills for content writers, content marketers, copywriters, and marketing professionals. Skills are grouped by marketing domain so you can quickly find what applies to your current projects.

The Skills Library contains dozens of content, brand, and marketing skills across the `strategy`, `ai-agents`, and `creative` categories. This guide highlights the top picks in each subcategory.

---

## Content Strategy

Skills for planning, creating, and measuring content that drives business results.

| Skill | Category | Description |
|-------|----------|-------------|
| `content-marketing` | strategy | Editorial planning, content creation frameworks, distribution channels, and ROI measurement |
| `content-performance` | strategy | Content analytics, engagement metrics, and data-driven optimization recommendations |
| `content-research-writer` | creative | Collaborative writing with research, citations, hooks, outlines, and real-time feedback |
| `thought-leadership` | strategy | Content strategy, publication management, and market positioning through expertise |

### Suggested Workflow: Launching a Content Program

1. Define your editorial mission and calendar with `content-marketing`
2. Use `content-research-writer` to research topics and draft high-quality articles
3. Position key voices with `thought-leadership` for bylines and speaking opportunities
4. Measure results and iterate with `content-performance` analytics

## Cross-Domain Workflows

These workflows combine skills from multiple sections above for common content marketing scenarios.

### Content-Led Product Launch

1. `content-marketing` -- Plan the editorial calendar around launch milestones
2. `storytelling` -- Develop the launch narrative and key messages
3. `copywriting` -- Write landing pages, ads, and promotional copy
4. `seo-content` -- Create search-optimized blog posts and pillar pages
5. `email-copywriting` -- Build announcement and drip email sequences
6. `social-content` -- Produce platform-specific social posts
7. `content-performance` -- Track engagement and conversion across channels

### Brand Voice Overhaul

1. `brand-identity` -- Revisit visual and verbal identity foundations
2. `brand-guidelines` -- Rewrite voice, tone, and messaging documentation
3. `style-guides` -- Update writing standards for the new voice
4. `brand-compliance` -- Audit existing content against new standards
5. `brand-tracking` -- Measure perception shifts after rollout

### Thought Leadership Program

1. `thought-leadership` -- Define positioning, topics, and publication targets
2. `content-research-writer` -- Research and draft long-form articles
3. `seo-optimization` -- Optimize published content for search visibility
4. `social-media-marketing` -- Amplify content across social channels
5. `email-marketing` -- Distribute via newsletter to subscriber base
6. `content-performance` -- Measure reach, engagement, and lead generation
