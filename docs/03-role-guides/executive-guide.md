# Executive / Leadership Guide

This guide curates the most valuable skills for C-suite executives, VPs, directors, and senior leadership. Skills are grouped by strategic domain so you can quickly find what applies to your current leadership priorities.

The Skills Library contains dozens of strategy, governance, and leadership skills across the `strategy`, `ai-agents`, and `creative` categories. This guide highlights the top picks in each subcategory.

---

## Strategic Planning

Skills for setting organizational direction, defining objectives, and translating vision into executable plans.

| Skill | Category | Description |
|-------|----------|-------------|
| `ceo` | ai-agents | Chief Executive Officer strategic advisor for organizational leadership and decision-making |
| `strategic-planning` | strategy | Vision development, mission articulation, OKR frameworks, and strategic initiative management |
| `strategic-initiatives` | strategy | Cross-functional project orchestration, OKR tracking, and initiative management |
| `growth-strategy` | strategy | Growth lever identification, experimentation frameworks, and scaling methodologies |
| `business-modeling` | strategy | Business model design including revenue models, unit economics, and financial viability assessment |

### Suggested Workflow: Annual Strategic Planning Cycle

1. Assess current position and set long-range vision with `strategic-planning`
2. Use `business-modeling` to validate revenue assumptions and unit economics
3. Identify and prioritize growth levers with `growth-strategy`
4. Break strategy into trackable initiatives using `strategic-initiatives`
5. Apply `ceo` for ongoing strategic advisory and decision support

## Cross-Domain Workflows

These workflows combine skills from multiple sections above for common executive scenarios.

### Fundraising Round

1. `business-modeling` -- Validate unit economics and financial projections
2. `financial-reporting` -- Prepare auditable financial statements
3. `investor-relations` -- Build pitch deck and manage due diligence
4. `competitive-intelligence` -- Articulate competitive advantages
5. `board-reporting` -- Keep the board informed on progress and terms

### Strategic Acquisition

1. `competitive-intelligence` -- Identify acquisition targets and strategic fit
2. `market-analysis` -- Size the market opportunity the target serves
3. `m-and-a` -- Execute due diligence and negotiate terms
4. `financial-reporting` -- Model combined financial impact
5. `executive-communication` -- Announce the acquisition internally and externally
6. `strategic-initiatives` -- Track integration milestones post-close

### Annual Operating Plan

1. `strategic-planning` -- Set annual objectives and key results
2. `growth-strategy` -- Identify the growth levers to invest in
3. `business-modeling` -- Model scenarios and resource requirements
4. `pricing-optimization` -- Refine pricing for revenue targets
5. `competitive-benchmarking` -- Validate targets against industry benchmarks
6. `board-reporting` -- Present the plan for board approval
