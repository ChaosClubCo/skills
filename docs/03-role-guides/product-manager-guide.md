# Product Manager Guide

This guide curates the most valuable skills for product managers, product owners, and growth leads. Whether you are defining strategy, running discovery, or analyzing metrics, these skills provide structured frameworks and best practices for every stage of the product lifecycle.

---

## Product Strategy and Planning

Core skills for defining what to build, why, and when.

| Skill | Category | Description |
|-------|----------|-------------|
| `product-strategy` | strategy | Defining product vision, goals, and strategic direction |
| `product-discovery` | ai-agents | Running discovery processes to validate problems and solutions |
| `product-launches` | strategy | Planning and executing product launches with go-to-market coordination |
| `go-to-market` | strategy | Go-to-market strategy including positioning, channels, and launch sequencing |
| `feature-prioritization` | technical | Frameworks for prioritizing features including RICE, MoSCoW, and weighted scoring |
| `feature-planning` | ai-agents | Detailed feature specification, scoping, and planning |
| `value-proposition` | strategy | Crafting compelling value propositions and positioning statements |
| `business-modeling` | strategy | Business model design including revenue models, cost structures, and unit economics |
| `strategic-planning` | strategy | Long-term strategic planning with OKRs, vision, and roadmaps |
| `strategic-initiatives` | strategy | Managing strategic initiatives from concept through execution |

### Suggested Workflow: Defining a New Product Direction

1. Assess the market with `market-analysis` and `competitive-intelligence`
2. Define your `value-proposition` and `business-modeling`
3. Set direction with `product-strategy` and `strategic-planning`
4. Validate with `product-discovery` before committing resources
5. Prioritize with `feature-prioritization` and plan the roadmap

## Discovery and Research

Skills for understanding users, markets, and competitive landscapes.

| Skill | Category | Description |
|-------|----------|-------------|
| `product-discovery` | ai-agents | Structured discovery processes including opportunity mapping and solution validation |
| `market-analysis` | strategy | Market sizing, segmentation, and trend analysis |
| `competitive-intelligence` | strategy | Gathering and analyzing competitor strategies, products, and positioning |
| `competitive-benchmarking` | strategy | Benchmarking products and features against competitors |
| `customer-analytics` | strategy | Analyzing customer behavior, segments, and lifetime value |
| `customer-segmentation` | strategy | Defining and analyzing customer segments for targeting |
| `customer-feedback` | ai-agents | Collecting, organizing, and acting on customer feedback |
| `product-feedback` | strategy | Processing product feedback from multiple channels into actionable insights |
| `audience-research` | ai-agents | Researching target audiences including demographics, behaviors, and needs |
| `cohort-analysis` | strategy | Analyzing user cohorts to understand retention, engagement, and revenue trends |
| `research-methods` | strategy | Qualitative and quantitative research methodologies |
| `design-research` | ai-agents | User research methods including interviews, surveys, and contextual inquiry |
| `ux-research` | ai-agents | UX research planning, execution, and synthesis |

### Suggested Workflow: Running Product Discovery

1. Define the problem space with `product-discovery`
2. Research users with `audience-research` and `design-research`
3. Analyze the market with `market-analysis` and `competitive-intelligence`
4. Validate with `customer-feedback` and `ux-research`
5. Synthesize findings into `cohort-analysis` and `customer-segmentation`

## Metrics and Analytics

Skills for measuring product performance and making data-driven decisions.

| Skill | Category | Description |
|-------|----------|-------------|
| `product-metrics` | technical | Defining and tracking product metrics including activation, engagement, and retention |
| `kpi-frameworks` | strategy | Building KPI hierarchies and measurement frameworks |
| `saas-metrics` | strategy | SaaS-specific metrics including MRR, churn, LTV, and CAC |
| `analytics-reporting` | strategy | Building reports and dashboards for regular business reviews |
| `business-dashboards` | strategy | Designing executive and operational dashboards |
| `dashboard-creator` | ai-agents | Creating interactive dashboards with modern visualization tools |
| `forecasting-models` | strategy | Building forecasting models for revenue, growth, and demand |
| `operational-analytics` | strategy | Analyzing operational data for efficiency and process improvements |
| `attribution-modeling` | ai-agents | Marketing and product attribution modeling for channel effectiveness |
| `reporting-automation` | ai-agents | Automating recurring reports and data pipelines |

### Suggested Workflow: Building a Product Metrics Framework

1. Define core metrics with `product-metrics` and `kpi-frameworks`
2. If SaaS, layer in `saas-metrics` for subscription-specific tracking
3. Build dashboards with `dashboard-creator` or `business-dashboards`
4. Set up `analytics-reporting` for regular stakeholder updates
5. Add `forecasting-models` for forward-looking projections

## Growth and Go-to-Market

Skills for driving adoption, retention, and revenue growth.

| Skill | Category | Description |
|-------|----------|-------------|
| `product-led-growth` | ai-agents | Product-led growth strategies including self-serve onboarding and viral loops |
| `growth-strategy` | strategy | Growth frameworks including acquisition, activation, retention, and referral |
| `go-to-market` | strategy | Go-to-market planning for new products and features |
| `product-launches` | strategy | Launch checklists, communication plans, and success criteria |
| `saas-pricing` | ai-agents | SaaS pricing strategies including packaging, tiers, and pricing psychology |
| `pricing-optimization` | strategy | Data-driven pricing optimization and experimentation |
| `churn-prevention` | ai-agents | Identifying and addressing churn risk factors |
| `customer-success-saas` | ai-agents | Customer success frameworks for SaaS retention and expansion |
| `saas-onboarding` | technical | Designing effective SaaS onboarding flows and activation milestones |
| `loyalty-programs` | ai-agents | Designing loyalty and rewards programs to drive retention |

### Suggested Workflow: Launching a New Feature

1. Define the launch plan with `product-launches` and `go-to-market`
2. Set pricing with `saas-pricing` or `pricing-optimization`
3. Build onboarding with `saas-onboarding` and `product-led-growth`
4. Track success with `product-metrics` and `cohort-analysis`
5. Reduce churn with `churn-prevention` and `customer-success-saas`
