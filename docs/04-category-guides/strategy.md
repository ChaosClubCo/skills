## Example Workflows

### Workflow 1: Launching a New Product Line

1. **market-analysis** -- Size the addressable market and map competitive landscape
2. **competitive-intelligence** -- Build battle cards and identify positioning gaps
3. **product-strategy** -- Define vision, roadmap, and portfolio fit
4. **value-proposition** -- Articulate unique value and differentiation
5. **go-to-market** -- Plan launch tiers, channels, and messaging
6. **pricing-optimization** -- Set pricing strategy and promotional plan
7. **product-launches** -- Coordinate cross-functional launch execution
8. **analytics-reporting** -- Configure dashboards to measure launch performance

### Workflow 2: Building an Annual Operating Plan

1. **strategic-planning** -- Set vision, mission, and OKR framework
2. **kpi-frameworks** -- Define balanced scorecard and metric hierarchy
3. **forecasting-models** -- Build revenue forecasts with scenario analysis
4. **resource-planning** -- Allocate team capacity across initiatives
5. **strategic-initiatives** -- Prioritize and assign cross-functional projects
6. **business-dashboards** -- Design executive dashboards for tracking
7. **meeting-facilitation** -- Establish quarterly business review cadence

### Workflow 3: Entering a New Market

1. **international-expansion** -- Assess market entry options and regulatory requirements
2. **market-analysis** -- Size TAM/SAM/SOM for the target geography
3. **competitive-benchmarking** -- Compare against local and global competitors
4. **customer-segmentation** -- Define ICP and personas for the new market
5. **go-to-market** -- Design market entry playbook
6. **content-marketing** -- Develop localized content strategy
7. **saas-metrics** -- Establish metrics to track market-specific performance
